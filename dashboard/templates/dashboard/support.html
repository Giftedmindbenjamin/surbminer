Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions
How long do withdrawals take?
Withdrawals are processed within 20 minutes. Network confirmation times vary by cryptocurrency.
What is the minimum deposit amount?
The minimum deposit amount varies by investment plan, starting from $100 for our Basic Plan.
How do I change my wallet address?
You can update your wallet addresses in the Profile section. Make sure to verify addresses before saving.
Is my investment secure?
Yes, we employ industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption and cold storage for cryptocurrencies.
How do referral commissions work?
You earn 5% commission on your referral's first deposit. Commissions are credited instantly to your account balance.
Can I have multiple accounts?
Yes, each user is not limited to one account. Multiple accounts are allowed.
